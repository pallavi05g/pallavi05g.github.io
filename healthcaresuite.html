Healthcare Suite Application Development

Project Description

Healthcare Suite for Rural India with a host of in-built services

Project Details

  • When: Sophomore year | R V College of Engineering | 2012
  • Where: Oracle ThinkQuest International Application Development Contest 2012
  • Position: Top 10% in the world among ~50000 participants
  • Role: Application Development
  • Project Type: Team of 4
  • Time frame: 12 weeks

Problem

  • Lack of basic health care facilities in rural areas
  • Low economic background
  • Lack of awareness
  • Urban doctors – expensive consultation

Our Solution

After days of brainstorming, we designed and developed a healthcare solution suite which comprised of a unique Symptom Checker, Home Remedy Assistant, array of health calculators, Women's Health Awareness Education Portal, Nearest Doctor Search, Children’s Vaccine Alert system. During the course of this project, we interacted with doctors and patients to understand the existing problem in rural healthcare. Our idea was simple: To ensure access to healthcare solutions for rural Indians, in the absence of medical practitioners.
