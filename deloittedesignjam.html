Zingerman Deli's Challenge
Data Visualizations for helping managers in day-to-day routine

Zingerman Deli's Challenge Design Jam

Project Description

Data visualizations to help managers plan their resources such as time, staff, material and costs on a day-to-day basis at Zingerman Deli's

Project Details

  • When: Sept 2016
  • Client: Deloitte Digital | University of Michigan School of Information
  • Role: UX Designer
  • Project Type: Team of 5
  • Time frame: 2 hours

Problem

There has been increasing number of customers at Zingerman's and managers want to ensure that they can handle the crowd in a smooth manner and serving each customer in a shorter span of time. They want all their data to be visualized on a dashboard that will help them work in a much more efficient manner.

Questions to be addressed

How many loaves of bread will need to be ordered from the bakery for it to last for the entire day?

How many customers are expected (daily/weekly/monthly) basis?

How many employees of each position will need to be staffed?

Av.order time is 1 min. How many employees must be staffed to ensure a fast moving line throughout the day? Where should they be?

Target Solution

We came up with a set of ideas that can be implemented to address the problem. We identified contraints and mapped the Zingerman Deli's space to understand what matters and how efficiency can be improved. Since Zingerman's becomes extremely crowded during game days, we decided to focus on that aspect as well.
