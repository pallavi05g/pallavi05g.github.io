About

Hello there!

I am Pallavi, an engineer-turned designer based in San Francisco Bay Area with a passion to change the way people interact with technology and simplify complex design problems. I am also an artist, a calligrapher, a travel enthusiast and a dessert lover.

Ever since childhood, I have been fascinated by the field of science. At the age of seven, building solar panels and lanterns with my entrepreneurial father ignited the maker spirit in me. On the other hand, watching my mother bake beautiful cakes, creating origami pieces and solving puzzles with her has crafted my creative side.

Majoring in Computer Science during undergrad was really exciting as it opened a world of opportunities. Presenting my hacks or proof-of-concept ideas on stage at various hackathons taught me problem solving techniques.

My experience as a Software Engineer at Samsung Research on Samsung Bixby introduced me to conversational voice assistants. This was a turning point in my journey as it piqued my curiosity about how humans really interact with computers. I went back to study Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, which turned out to be one amazing set of experiences. Go Blue!

Today, at Sumo Logic, I focus on enriching the user experience for information-heavy data products such as dashboards, queries and security analytics.

When I'm not designing, I'm either traveling

Or looking for the next best dessert

Or sketching
