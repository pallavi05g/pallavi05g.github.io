Interactive Storytelling through gamification of cultural folklore

STOReeFY Interaction Design Project

Project Description

Do you want to see what play a character within a story? Do you know what Interactive Storytelling is? Are you interested in gaming? Do you want to have a unique experience?

STOReeFY introduces an interactive way of listening to age-old stories. What do we have for you?

  • Wide collection of interactive stories to choose from
  • Curated story albums created from in-game tasks within the interactive story
  • Popular stories based on what your peers are engaging with

Project Details

  • When: Sept 2016 to Present
  • Where: University of Michigan, Ann Arbor
  • Role: Interaction Designer
  • Project Type: Individual
  • Time frame: 12 weeks
