Facebook Photo Upload Feature Challenge Design Jam
Making the photo upload process smoother on Facebook



Description

The challenge was to sketch/prototype/improve the photo retrieval and upload processo on Facebook. Additionally, we had to create a detailed plan that would understand prevalence of this issue and measure success of a redesign.

Details

  • When: Oct 2016
  • Client: Facebook | University of Michigan School of Information
  • Role: UX Designer
  • Project Type: Team of 6
  • Time frame: 3 hours

Problem

Photo upload process on Facebook is one of the crucial aspects of the social networking site. We had to break down the problem given into three - mobile, website, app. In each of them, we had to understand aspects of upload and retrieval.

Issues Identified

1. Mobile
2. Website
3. App
