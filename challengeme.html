Challenge Me Hackathon
Productivity + Fun = Awesomeness!

Project Description

A unique concept of defining fun with a productivity app. This concept gamifies the concept of restricted mobile usage via challenges. The social aspect to the game makes it all the more interesting.

Project Details

  • When: March 6th, 2015 – March 8th, 2015
  • Where: International Women’s Day Hackathon 2015 | HackerEarth - Top 36th team in the world
  • Position: Top 1% among 10000 women developers around the world
  • Role: Conceptualization | Sketching | Brainstorming | PoC
  • Project Type: Team of 2
  • Time frame: 2 days

Problem

We all love challenges as it helps us get better. We like aiming for the stars. However, we humans are fickle minded creatures. We are lazy and don’t bother about anything till something is innovative. However, we get hooked onto things and we have no control over ourselves. We tend to become technology addicts. Pokemon Go was a craze. What about Candy Crush? We love scrolling through Faceboo or Twitter feed. Any social media for that matter. Is there a way we can manage it all well? This was the question that we were pondering about and decided to add an element of fun to screen time.

Target Audience

Anyone who wants to manage screen time but just cannot and likes challenges and social gaming

Process Diagram

Our Proposal



Challenge your friends for our unique app challenges on your mobile device. Here are some of the key features of this game:

  • See who has a better sense of self control
  • Fun way to stay away from your phone
  • Set time durations and if you still manage to stay away from your phone, you win points
  • Exchange point with trophies
  • Share the scores with your friends and see who wins
  • Get notified if your friends use their phone despite the challenge
