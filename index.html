About


Hello there! I am Pallavi, an engineer-turned designer based in San Francisco Bay Area with a passion to create intuitive product experiences that change the way people interact with technology.

As a curious kid who personified every little object, I wanted objects to talk to me. It was pure serendipity that I ended up working on a voice assistant at Samsung Research.

I continued to be curious about conceptualizing talking objects. That’s when I decided to go back to school to study how humans really interact with computers.

Today, I love anything that has to do with tangible interfaces, data storytelling and social computing. Apart from design, I love photography, cooking, doodling, writing, traveling and experimenting with different cuisines.

Curious to learn more? Shoot me an email at pallavig@umich.edu
