Hi, I'm Pallavi! 👋

I'm an engineer-turned-designer with 12+ years in tech, most of it spent making AI technology feel human, not just intelligent.

I started as a software engineer at Samsung, building the NLP infrastructure behind Bixby, work that reached 10M+ users across 8 languages. That technical grounding is what pulled me toward design. After my Master's in Human-Computer Interaction, I shifted my focus to the human side of these complex systems.

Since then, I've worked the full spectrum of AI product design at Amazon — from 0→1 launches like Alexa+ (Amazon's generative AI assistant) and a multimodal language-learning experience that reached 250K+ monthly users, to owning 1→N product discovery on the Amazon Shopping app, driving $600M+ in sales across 350M+ users globally. Earlier, I helped take Sumo Logic from Series C to a $2B+ IPO, redesigning its query builder to cut query time in half and lift NPS by 20 points.

I've operated at massive-scale companies and lived a startup's full arc to IPO, so I move comfortably between ambiguity and structure. I'm open to exploring Staff/Principal design leadership roles at companies building what's next in AI.
