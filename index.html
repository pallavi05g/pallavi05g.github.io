Power Optimization for phones and tablets

About

Analytical tool and Linux kernel module to under and optimize power consumed by various components in mobile devices

Project description

This project involved in-depth research about power consumption in an application processor and identification of loopholes in mobile application development. As a part of this project, I developed a Linux kernel debug module for power optimization in Linux kernel version 3.4. Additionally, I developed a C# data visualization tool to understand the behavior of various internal parameters such as C-States, P-States, Interrupts and Operating Frequencies for mobile devices. By the end of the project, we were able to optimize power usage by 40% in one of the use cases, based on the graphs generated by this visual tool.

ASHA: Sapnon Ki Jholi

About

Asha - Sapno Ki Jholi was born when we started discussing about the energy crisis in the city and how we could make a difference. In Hindi, "Asha" means hope and "Sapno Ki Jholi" means a cradle of dreams. This project was an effort in the direction of raising awareness about waste management. The notion behind Asha was to promote civic sense and environmental responsibility and foster innovative ways to inculcate the awareness about waste segregation and management solutions.

Our Proposal

The solution that we developed comprised of the various components which would together result in a cost effective solution. It consisted of a user friendly application in the form of a game which would teach children about the various aspects of waste management; a fact randomiser which would deal with concepts related to civic sense, waste disposal, segregation; picture filled presentations which would cover harmful effects of dumping and inefficient waste disposal; a newsletter that would demonstrate segregation success stories, community involvement and benefits and 3R (Reduce, Reuse, Recycle) bins. The whole solution was designed keeping in mind rural India as the target audience.

Challenge Me

About

Productivity + Fun = Awesomeness! A unique concept of defining fun with a productivity app. Humans are fickle minded creatures. They are lazy and don’t bother about anything till something is innovative. We all like to restrict our mobile usage. We try hard to not use Facebook for prolonged hours. We don’t want to become Candy Crush addicts. Yet, we can’t stay away from it all. We all love challenges. Why not, add these elements to productivity apps? An element of fun? Challenge? Scores?

Our Proposal

Challenge your friends for our unique app challenges on your mobile device. Here are some of the key features of this game:

  • See who has a better sense of self control
  • Fun way to stay away from your phone
  • Set time durations and if you still manage to stay away from your phone, you win points
  • Exchange point with trophies
  • Share the scores with your friends and see who wins
  • Get notified if your friends use their phone despite the challenge

Blood Search

About

We envisioned a platform to connect blood donors and recipients. Though the current system of blood banks exists, we wanted to enhance the experience as the whole process of blood donation isn’t organized well and is cumbersome.

Our Proposal

Our system envisages an application, which allows users to sign up, locate, search, learn and share. A user, on registration, creates a profile, which notes the blood type, location and other personal details. The user can then search our database for the number of blood units requested along with the blood type. Our solution searches this query against our user database and suggests nearby people who are willing to donate. The donors are notified in case of a request, which they can accept, using a simple interface. In cases where no nearby donors are present, our application notifies the nearest blood bank. The uniqueness in our application lies in the fact that we create a unified platform for both individual users and blood banks.

Drive Smart

About

Traffic has always been an issue in Bangalore and it is tiresome to commute in the city. We wanted to leverage technology to change this.

Our Proposal

We proposed a solution to get rid of the traffic nightmare in the city with an efficient traffic management system. Our proposal revolved around live traffic updates based on location and intensity of the jam. It covered aspects such as road diversions, roadblocks due to festivities and celebrations. The solution was targeted at users who wanted to avoid possible areas of congestion. It was designed to be a crowdsourcing kind of a system where we visualized a user based input system integrated with social media integration and GPS systems, thus enabling a real-time live traffic feed. This solution could equip emergency services like fire brigades and ambulances to navigate better within the city.

Auto Answer

About

The idea that inspired us to develop this app was simple - ‘Have you ever been worried about a friend or a family member when they don’t pick up your call?’

Our Proposal

This Android mobile application enables users to send customized messages to groups of contacts incase the person misses answering his/her cellphone. There are high chances that users would like to send different text messages based on the person who calls them, where the user isn't able to reject the call with a text. By allowing the users to create custom groups and messages which can be preset and an intuitive user interface for custom message and group creation, this app managed to find a better use case than the traditional "reject call with a text" approach.
